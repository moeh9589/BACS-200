DISCS

Everything you need to know about discs

Hover over the image to zoom

----------------------------

Every disc is different. Every disc has a different shape and a different weight. Finding the right disc is a matter of personal preference as every disc will behave differently. It might not seem like much, but the little differences in shape and weight can cause a disc to go a completely different way. Each disc has 4 numbers on it(or they should). These 4 numbers correspond to speed, glide, fade and turn. More detail on those will be down below.

Hover over the image to shake

----------------------------

Speed

The first number on each disc corresponds ot its speed. This number ranges from 0-14. The higher the speed, the easier it is to make the disc fly faster. This does not necessarily mean that you should go out and buy every disc that has a 14. This is because some discs with high speeds are harder to make them do what you want. As for beginners, discs with speeds from 7-10 are easy to throw and make for good starter discs.
